Project Title

Better Sights with Eyetracker

Project Details

Purpose of Better Sights with Eyetracker aims to assist users interact with different computer interfaces. a. Program helps people with poor eyesight to see something clearer. b. Patients can use their eyes to control computer. c. Program helps people with reading and comprehension through the use of highlights to attract eye movement. d. Patients who is recovering after a eye surgery can use eyetracker to train eye movements e. People will be recommended more useful information according to their preference, so they could pay more attention to what they really like.
