Project Title

Better Sights with Eyetracker

Project Details

Imagine you are reading through a web page, and the font is too small to read. Your first reaction may be to squint your eyes, as it's much more convenient than zoooming in with the mouse. What if your computer was able to detect this and magnify the display in response? This is what we aim to do.

The purpose of our project aims to assist users who have trouble reading small text on web pages. We plan to integrate this function directly into a browser. Therefore, the steps for this project are as follows:

  • detect the movement of people's eyes with eye tracker;
  • detect and analyzing the shape of eyelids to see if the eyes are narrowed;
  • use eyes instead of hands to control the computer;
  • Magnifying the content on the web pages if there is a signal showing people are narrowing their eyes.
